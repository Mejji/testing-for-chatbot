Blessed Giorgio Frassati Building

The Blessed Pier Giorgio Frassati Building of the University of Santo Tomas was formally inaugurated on December 10, 2021. It currently houses the UST Senior High School, and the College of Information of Computing Sciences (CICS). It stands between Extramadura Street and Cayco Street.

College of Information and Computing Sciences (CICS)

The College occupies the Seventeenth floor and onwards of the building, and its office is currently located in the Second floor.
